Dedan Wachira (Mr. dee), is an art designer + painter in Kenya, with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Kenyatta University. He also opened an art studio at Kings College that ran successfully for 5 years, and did two major events, Haystacks & Jazz. To find out more contact him today.

My artistic thinking process

  Inspiration

Through research, observation, personal experience and self expression, I decide what I have to make.

  Development

I make the idea visual in the best wau that I can through sketches and experimenting.

  Creation

The third stage of my process is to apply what I've developed into a successful artwork.

  Reflection & Presentation

Lastly, I analyze and collect information for revision, give and receive feedback, and present my artwork.
